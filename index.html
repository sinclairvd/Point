MAP band

Home
MAP BAND is a contemporary Praise and Worship group from The Netherlands. They have their own unique sound and songs. Micha Maasbach is the lead vocal and writer of the lyrics and music. There are three other singers that accompany him and a four men band. The ‘Make A Point’ Band has performed at numerous occasions in The Netherlands, Switzerland and The Philippines.
Although their music is geared towards the youth it attracts people of all ages. Their songs includes up beat and intimate praise. Every concert is an experience that stays in the mind and heart.

Order now their release album (DVD/CD) on Bijbelhuis.nl
Also available on iTunes & Spotify
